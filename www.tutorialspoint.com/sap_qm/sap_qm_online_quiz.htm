Q 1 - Which of the following module is integrated with QM to manage the releases for vendors and manufacturers and also monitor QM systems for suppliers?

A - SAP Material Management

B - SAP Production Planning

C - SAP Sales and Distribution

D - SAP Controlling

Answer : A

Explanation

You can integrate following tasks of SAP MM with QM −

  • You manage the quality details for materials, vendors and suppliers.

  • You manage the releases for vendors and manufacturers and also monitor QM systems for suppliers.

  • You manage the release status of delivery from vendor.

  • You can maintain quality agreements and RFP details when you request for a quotations and purchase orders is raised for vendors.

  • You can manage quality certificates for vendors.

Q 2 - You can assign an inspection method to master inspection charactertics or directly to an inspection charactertics in an inspection plan?

A - True

B - False

Answer : A

Explanation

You can use inspection method to perform the inspection. An inspection method can be assigned to master inspection charactertics or directly to an inspection charactertics in an inspection plan.

An Inspection method can be created in one plant same method can be used in other plant.

Q 3 - A sampling plan defines the sample size specification for the inspection lot to determine if a sample is accepted or not accepted?

A - True

B - False

Answer : A

Q 4 - While defining a sampling procedure, which of the following make up inspection charactertics?

A - Sampling Type

B - Valuation Mode

C - Inspection Method Status

D - A & B

E - All of the above

Answer : D

Explanation

The rules for determining the sample are stored in the sampling type. The sampling type and valuation mode are combined for the inspection characteristics.

Q 5 - What do you understand by AQL?

A - Acceptable Quality Limit

B - Automatic Quality Length

C - Acceptable Quality Length

D - Automatic Quality Limit

Answer : A

Explanation

AQL (Acceptable Quality Level) is the quality level that represents the upper limit of a satisfactory average quality level for an acceptance sampling inspection.

Q 6 - What is SAP inspection type used for in process material inspection?

A - 01

B - 02

C - 03

D - 04

Answer : C

Q 7 - What is the standard SAP QM notification type to record a complaint against vendor and take action for the incoming material?

A - Q1

B - Q2

C - Q3

D - Not Defined

Answer : B

Explanation

You can use standard SAP QM notification type Q2 to record a complaint against vendor and take action for the incoming material.

Q 8 - For costing and to decide how many goods are accepted, SAP QM is directly linked to?

A - SAP MM

B - SAP PP

C - Controlling

D - SAP Plant Maintenance

Answer : C

Explanation

SAP QM is directed linked to controlling for costing and to decide how many goods are accepted. You update the vendor quality record in the system and this helps to find out if same vendor should continue to supply the material or there is a need to find a new source.

Q 9 - Which of the following document types are considered as Procurement document in procurement cycle?

A - Quality Certificates

B - Quality Assurance document Q01

C - Quality Purchase Order QP1

Answer : B

Q 10 - Which of the following can be used to adjust the probability of acceptance and the inspection effort, based on different quality situations?

A - Inspection Severity

B - AQL

C - Sample Size

D - All of the above

Answer : A

Explanation

In SAP Quality Management process, you can vary inspection severity that allows to adjust the probability of acceptance and the inspection effort, based on different quality situations. The inspection severity is closely connected to the inspection scope. If all other conditions remain the same, different inspection severities lead to different inspection scopes or acceptance conditions.
